Services

Our comprehensive services are geared towards empowering users to effortlessly track their shipments and make necessary adjustments as needed. One of the key features that sets ExPost apart is the dedicated "Services" column, where we offer a range of functionalities to enhance your shipping experience.

1. Shipment Details: In the Services column, you can easily input your shipment details, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information for your logistics. Whether it's a new shipment or a modification to an existing one, ExPost streamlines the process, putting you in control of your deliveries.

2. Live Location Tracking: Our cutting-edge technology provides live location updates for your shipments. Through the Services section, you can access real-time tracking information, allowing you to monitor the progress of your package from origin to destination.

3. Address Modification: Need to change the delivery address? No problem. ExPost allows you to modify the address of your shipment effortlessly. The Services column simplifies the address adjustment process, giving you the flexibility to ensure your package reaches the right destination.

4. Shipment Deletion: Circumstances change, and sometimes you need to cancel a shipment. With ExPost's Services feature, you can easily delete a shipment, providing you with the control and flexibility you need to manage your logistics effectively.
